User can Pass multiple queries seperated by comma at once. (sports, kohli)

By default several predefined params will be passed along with custom params to make sure results will be relevent to india. Unchecking that option will wipe predefined params, and that will search news all over the world.

Search scopes tells API where to search for queries. There are 2 Options available. searching from title will give less results but that results will be more relavent to the query searched. On the other hand searching from summary will give user more results but those results may not that much relavent to query searched. choose option according to your need.
